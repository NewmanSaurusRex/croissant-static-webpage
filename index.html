Fact: There is No So Thing as a Yummy Gluten-Free Croissant 🥐

Gluten is a 💪 protein 💪 found in wheat, rye and barley plants.

Gluten reactions naturally occur in nature, but we can also extract it, concentrate it and add it to food (or other products) as a protein supplement, to create varied textures and/or flavors.

The structure of a 🥐croissant🥐 depends on gluten!

When wheat flour is mixed with water, gluten proteins chemically react to create the croissant's perfect buttery, flaky consistency!

My Texan grandmother would tell you that gluten "makes food taste good.👵🏼🍪"
