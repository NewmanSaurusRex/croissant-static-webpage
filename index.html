Fact: There is No So Thing as a Yummy Gluten-Free Croissant 🥐

Gluten is a 💪 protein 💪 found in wheat, rye and barley plants. It is naturally occurring, but it can also be extracted, concentrated and added to food (and other products) as a protein supplement, to create varied textures and/or flavors.

Gluten also works as a binding agent to hold processed foods together and give them shape. The structure of a 🥐croissant🥐 depends on gluten! When wheat flour is mixed with water, gluten proteins chemically react to create the croissant's perfect buttery, flaky consistency!

My Texan grandmother would tell you that gluten "makes food taste good.👵🏼🍪"
